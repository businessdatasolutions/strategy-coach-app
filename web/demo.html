Research-Backed Methodologies

Reference: "Making Great Strategy" (2021)

The Logic Agent validates strategic reasoning using principles from behavioral economics and decision science.

Reference: "Thinking, Fast and Slow" (2011)

Question design follows research on avoiding cognitive biases in questionnaires for more authentic insights.

Reference: "A Catalog of Biases in Questionnaires" (2005)
